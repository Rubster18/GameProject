Choose your class!

Warrior

Warriors have high health, damage and defense, however their sturdy armor makes them somewhat slow.

Rogue

Rogues are nimble and fast, they can attack many times making them very deadly, the downside is that because of their light armor, their health is low and their damage are not as high as other classes.

Archer

Archers are precise and deadly, they can't attack many times but when they hit they hit hard, they speed and health are well balanced.

Mage

By converting mana into devastating spells the mages can decimate foes, they lack many combat stats but they can make it up by ending fights in secconds.
